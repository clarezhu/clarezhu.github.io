Vita

I was born in Iowa and hail originally from Shrewsbury, MA.

I hold a Bachelor's as well as a Master's degree in Computer Science which I completed coterminally at Stanford. During that time, I also completed a minor in Mathematics. My coursework consisted of primarily machine learning, followed by theory and algorithms.

In the past ten years, I've counted these cities among my homes: Irvine (Ca.), San Diego, Baltimore, Beijing, Palo Alto, Fairfield (Conn.), Chicago, Boston, New York City, and Miami.
