Why?

One of my favorite things I learned in school was Prof. Vakil's "Three Things" exercise for getting things out of talks: The theory is as follows. If you can get even three small things out of a talk, it is a successful talk. And if you can't get even three small things out of a talk, it was not a successful experience.

(In this page, "talk" is interchanged rather generously with "stuff I liked or found memorable".)

Three things from 2025

  • A Ukrainian cafe made me one of these raf lattes and now I have a chemical dependence on them.
  • The Expanse (2015), hooked from the first flip and burn.
  • Words of wisdom from one of the greatest teachers and most profound influences on my life.
